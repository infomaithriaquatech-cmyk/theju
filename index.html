FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about TAPL's products, capabilities, and partnerships.

What battery systems does TAPL manufacture?+
TAPL manufactures Li-Ion battery systems from 12.8V to 96V, 6Ah to 120Ah in LFP and NMC chemistries. Our range covers EV batteries (60V/45Ah), solar tracker batteries (14.4V/3.2Ah), medical ventilator batteries (14.4V/7.5Ah), defence HAA batteries (28.8V/120Ah), and containerized BESS from 1–5 MWh with our proprietary 5S–256S Smart BMS.
Does TAPL supply to defence organisations?+
Yes. Our Defence Division engineers mission-critical power systems to military-grade standards. Products include Li-Ion batteries for Long Range Telescope (LRT) systems, AFV sub-system power, torpedo BLDC motor controllers (3kW), and ruggedised BMS platforms for High Altitude Area (HAA) and Extreme Cold Climate (ECC) environments.
What are TAPL's hybrid inverter specifications?+
TAPL offers the TT1000VA (1kVA/800W, 25.6V battery), IG2000VA (2kVA/1600W, 48V battery), and TT3000VA (3kVA/2400W, 48V battery). All feature: Pure Sine Wave output, MPPT solar charging, UPS & Inverter modes, Solar/Grid priority, LCD display, and 4kV surge protection. Operating temperature: 0–50°C.
What is the TASoil+ sensor and who is it for?+
TASoil+ is a research-grade, rugged, buriable soil moisture and temperature sensor with ±2% accuracy, 8-second response time, 4–20mA output, and 15–30VDC operation. Designed for soil science, horticulture, precision irrigation, environmental research, sports turf management, and golf courses.
Can TAPL do custom or co-development projects?+
Absolutely. Co-development and OEM partnerships are central to TAPL's model. We engage in joint development of battery systems, embedded controllers, power electronics, and IoT platforms. We offer hardware/software IP licensing, white-labeled products, and long-term technology partnerships.
What is CUBEX and when does it launch?+
CUBEX is India's first portable power generator — a compact station that recharges 0 to 100% in just 2 hours, powering up to 5 devices simultaneously. Perfect for drones, outdoor work, camping, and power outages. Launching November 5, 2025.
